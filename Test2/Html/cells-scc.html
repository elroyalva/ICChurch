SMALL CHRISTIAN COMMUNITIES or SCCs as is popularly called aims at a significant shift in the style of functioning in the Church from the erstwhile hierarchical, clergy led dispensation with the laity accustomed to pray, pay and obey towards a collaborative movement where the laity increasingly become active participants, willing to share in the mission of the Church and to take on responsibilities. The purpose of the SCCs is to look after the
a)material needs;
b) the human growth and
c) the spiritual growth of its members;
in other words, the holistic development of every Catholic.

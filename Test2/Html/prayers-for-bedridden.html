Prayer for the Bedridden

Lord of mercy and of love, you know the weakness and fears of Your servants; in Your mercy, give strength to the bedridden who do not enjoy the freedom of walking about and are dependent on others for so much. When they are frustrated give them courage and when in despair, give them hope. May they always know Your Presence and find purpose and meaning in life and rejoice in Your love; through Jesus Christ our Lord. Amen.

Our Lady of

Immaculate Conception Church,

Borivali West

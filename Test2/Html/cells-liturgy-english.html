The members prepare and conduct the Liturgy during Advent, Lent, Feast Days and special occasions and are lectors or cantors for daily Masses. Regular practice helps the lectors to proclaim the Word of God and not just read it. The group coordinates and collaborates with sectors needing help in preparing and conducting the liturgy. The liturgy is enhanced with choirs at every Mass.

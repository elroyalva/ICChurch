Neighbourhood Prayer

May God's love, power and peace flow from us to our neighbour.
Come Holy Spirit we need Thee.
Come Holy Spirit we pray.
Come in thy strength and power, come in thy own gentle way.
Come as wisdom to children; come as insight to the grown.
Come lord as rest to the weary, fill us with joy ever more.
Amen.

Our Lady of

Immaculate Conception Church,

Borivali West

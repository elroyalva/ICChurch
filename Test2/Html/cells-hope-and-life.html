It is a support group for widows to help them accept widowhood as a vocation, gain emotional stability and help them become prayerful, joyful and helpful. The movement is a blend of spiritually, fellowship, outreach and support. They organize retreats, recollections, visits to shrines, aged homes or orphanages, a Christmas party and a yearly picnic.

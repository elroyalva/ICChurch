Prayer before Meetings

O Lord, Eternal God, we are about to begin our meeting, and we do so with the awareness that without Your Divine Presence here at the center of our meeting, and also within ourselves, our work will be empty.

Grace us with Your Wisdom and Vision; gift us with Holy Humor and Humility so that not only this meeting but all our lives may be a meeting place for your Kingdom.

We ask this through our Lord, Jesus Christ, in the unity of the Holy Spirit, now and forever. Amen.

Our Lady of

Immaculate Conception Church,

Borivali West

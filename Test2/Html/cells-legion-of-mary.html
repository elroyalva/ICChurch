The objective is self-sanctification of its members and those with whom they come in contact through prayer and active apostolic works. The members reach out to people of all faiths by family visits, encouraging prayer and participation in parish and SCC activities, directing people needing help, visiting institutions, casual conversations with people on the streets, etc. They also work with SCC units in organizing certain programmes like parent-youth interaction. There is a senior Tamil unit, 5 senior English units and a English junior unit.

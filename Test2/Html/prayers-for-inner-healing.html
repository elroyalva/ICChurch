Prayer for Inner Healing

Lord Jesus Christ, we bring to you those who labour and are overburdened. Your power reaches to the hidden and unseen areas of our being. Touch the minds and heart of our brother/sister (name). Fill him/her with the strength of your Holy Spirit. Free him/her from anxiety, guilt and distress. Enable him/her to turn to you in faith and love and to receive your peace and joy, for you are Lord for ever and ever. Amen.

Our Lady of

Immaculate Conception Church,

Borivali West

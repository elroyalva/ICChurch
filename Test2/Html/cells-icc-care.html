IC Care is the community welfare fund of our parish. The fund is built up by regular contributions from parishioners and disbursed as follows. 50% for medical aid, education aid, rations and assistance to the needy on merit, 10% is allotted to each sector for community building activities, 5% in a pool to assist poorer sectors, 10% as reserves for emergency/contingent use, 20% to the Church for support/facilities/amenities provided and for usual maintenance and 5% for Development Fund to meet recreational and other expenditure for parishioners.

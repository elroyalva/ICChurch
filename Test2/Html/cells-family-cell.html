This Cell works for the spiritual and emotional growth of families, by inviting renowned resource persons to conduct various sessions for Couples, Single Parents and Mixed Married couples. They initiated the annual Family Day Programme for the entire parish. They also initiated Kids Alive, a programme of faith formation every alternate Saturday for children between the ages of 3 and 5. Sessions for the parents of these children are also regularly conducted.
